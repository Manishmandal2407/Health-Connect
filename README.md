Brief: A modern web-based solution that enables patients and healthcare providers to interact and manage health remotely.
- Developed a secure and scalable full-stack web platform enabling patients and healthcare professionals to manage health remotely.
- The system facilitated real-time video consultations, electronic health records access, appointment scheduling, and live chat significantly improving healthcare accessibility. 
- Tech Stack: MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, Socket.IO, JWT, WebRTC
- Reduced patient wait times by 60% through streamlined online appointment handling.
- Enabled access to healthcare for users in remote.
